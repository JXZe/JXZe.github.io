About Me

I'm a second-year Master student at School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering (SASEE), Beihang University (BUAA), supervised by Prof. Zengchang Qin. I am currently conducting an internship at Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) with the guidance of Prof. Jing Yu. I'm also lucky to have opportunities to collaborate with Qi Wu (Professor at Adelaide), Yaobo Liang (Researcher at MSRA), Xingxing Zhang (Researcher at MSRA). Before this, I got my Bachelor's degree in Automation Science from Northeast Forestry University (2014-2018).

My primary research interests are Multi-modal Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. Specifically, his current research focuses on vision and language, e.g. visual dialog, cross-lingual pre-training and image/video captioning.
